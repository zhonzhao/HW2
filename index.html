About Me

Hello everyone! Thanks for visiting my personal webpage.

I am an undergraduate student who is crazy about travel and outdoor activities, but sometimes, I am professional business student just like what you see in my profile picture.

I was born in China. By the age of 15, I selected as a scholar to study in Singapore under full scholarship and that was the first time I visited a foreign country. My amazing journey started at that time. I represented my high school to participate a basketball tournament in Malaysia in spring 2008. At the end of 2008, I visited South Korea with my family. During summer 2012 a participated CIMBA Italy program hosted by Tippie College of Business and visited 9 countries in Europe, including: Italy, Germany, France, Austria, Czech, Poland, Slovakia, Greece and Brussels. It was an amazing journey. I like Europe a lot since that summer. So I decided to visit Europe again in this summer. This time, I mainly focused on Scandinavia and outdoor activities.
